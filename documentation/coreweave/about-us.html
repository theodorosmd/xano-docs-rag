The AI Hyperscaler™ empowering the future

What we do

CoreWeave is a cloud purpose-built for scaling, supporting, and accelerating GenAI. We’re a comprehensive platform and strategic partner designed to tackle today—and tomorrow’s—challenges of deploying AI at scale. We manage the complexities of AI growth to make supercomputing accessible and push the limits of what’s possible. Our teams create modern solutions to support modern technology. Get the premier choice for working with GenAI workloads.

Our values

We believe in the power of people. As fast-movers and forward-thinkers, our values are the mantras behind why we do what we do.

Be curious at your core

We foster a mindset of continuous learning, understanding that curiosity drives innovation. We seek out information, question assumptions, and value different perspectives. Curiosity is a driving force that allows us to explore ideas and innovations that push boundaries, challenge the status quo, and better serve our customers.

Empower employees

We trust each other to make decisions, communicate transparently, and are clear on our goals. We support each other with the tools, resources, and feedback necessary to succeed. We know that employees can do their best work when they feel valued, supported, and heard.

Act like an owner

We take full responsibility for our work and our decisions to find the best solution to any challenge. Wait for others to solve problems? Not us – we step up, innovate, and drive progress. We take initiative, hold ourselves accountable, and foster a culture where everyone feels empowered to contribute to our success.

Deliver best-in-class experiences

Some might say we’re customer-obsessed. We go above and beyond to understand our customers needs and aim to exceed expectations at every step. Our commitment to excellence ensures we build lasting partnerships and set the standard for exceptional service.

Achieve more together

Collaboration is at the heart of our success. Together, we unlock greater potential and solve challenges more effectively. We believe that by leveraging diverse perspectives, supporting each other, and working as one team, we can accomplish more than we could ever do alone.
