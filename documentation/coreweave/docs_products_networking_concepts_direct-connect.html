Direct Connect

Connect directly to CoreWeave via private, dedicated links

Clients who need guaranteed capacity connections can connect to CoreWeave either directly or through our partners, Equinix ECX and Megaport.

Customers who use Equinix and Megaport can use their existing connections when establishing direct connections to CoreWeave. There is no requirement to add additional dedicated physical ports, or establish a network presence in our regions. For supported locations and available partner networks, see the tables below.

We also support low latency connections in select regions. All regions support MACsec encryption for secure data transmission.

Learn more

Please contact support to learn more about Direct Connect configuration, pricing, and the LOAs required to cross-connect to our edge networks.
