LlamaIndex Brand Guidelines

Guidelines for representing the LlamaIndex brand with clarity and consistency

Download Brand Assets

Brand Name

“LlamaIndex” is always written as a single word with a capital “L” and “I.” It serves as the brand name for both our company and product (not “LlamaIndex app” or variations).

When referencing specific features or releases, please capitalize them as proper nouns (e.g., “LlamaIndex Engine”).

Usage

Ensure there is ample space around LlamaIndex and LlamaCloud assets to maintain clarity and impact. Whether scaling them up or down, allow them to breathe—avoid making them feel cramped or cluttered. The logo should always be used on a black background to maintain brand consistency and visual integrity.

LlamaIndex Wordmark

The LlamaIndex wordmark should be used whenever referring to LlamaIndex, provided space allows.

LlamaIndex Logo

For compact layouts or logo-only displays, the LlamaIndex logomark offers a simplified way to represent the brand. Use it thoughtfully based on your audience, as the full LlamaIndex wordmark carries stronger brand recognition.

LlamaCloud Wordmark

The LlamaCloud wordmark should be used whenever referring to LlamaCloud, specifically in the context of our cloud platform.

Colors

LlamaIndex brand identity is defined by smooth gradient transitions, creating a dynamic and modern aesthetic. These gradients are designed to work seamlessly against both light and dark backgrounds.
