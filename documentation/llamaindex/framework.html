Production Ready Framework For LLM Agents

LlamaIndex is a simple, flexible framework for building agentic generative AI applications that allow large language models to work with your data in any format.

use-cases

From Simple to Advanced

Summarization

Harness the power of AI to distill complex texts into concise, easily digestible summaries. Our advanced algorithms can analyze large volumes of information, ranging from lengthy reports to sprawling data sets, and provide clear, succinct summaries. This tool is perfect for professionals who need to quickly grasp the essence of detailed documents or for anyone seeking to streamline their information intake.

Q&A/Search

The most advanced framework for any Q&A use case and data, from financial analysis to customer support to internal Slack messages. Explore dozens of exclusive techniques for advanced RAG use cases that aren’t available in any other framework.

Agents

Build LLM-powered agents that can perform complex workflows over your data and services. Program them to perform a wide range of tasks, from performing multi-document comparisons to automating your calendar to synthesizing code. Jumpstart your agent with our agent implementations + 30+ tool connectors in LlamaHub or easily write your own.

Loading

Load in 160+ data sources and data formats, from unstructured, semi-structured, to structured data (API's, PDF's, documents, SQL, etc.)

Explore more

Indexing

Store and index your data for different use cases. Integrate with 40+ vector store, document store, graph store, and SQL db providers.

Querying

Evaluating
